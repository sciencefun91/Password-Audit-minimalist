Intro

Introduction I chose to dedicate my final project to the theme of password security—an aspect often underestimated but fundamental in protecting computer systems. Passwords are indeed the primary authentication mechanism and, when managed inadequately, become one of the most exposed points to attacks. Improving their use thus means significantly reducing the risk surface for both users and services. With this in mind, the developed website integrates two complementary tools designed to be clear, reliable, and technically transparent: a function to verify credential exposure via the Have I Been Pwned API, and a configurable secure password generator. Both aim to promote more conscious and robust choices in the daily management of one’s digital credentials.
